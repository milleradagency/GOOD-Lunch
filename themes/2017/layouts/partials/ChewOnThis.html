Event Information

THE LUNCH aims to raise funds to support the mission of Goodwill Dallas, which is to provide job training and employment to persons with disabilities and disadvantages to help transform their lives through the power of jobs.

100% of THE LUNCH proceeds benefit individuals with disabilities and disadvantages, providing them with free job training and placement services. With your support of THE LUNCH, more people in our community are given a starting point, a pathway, an opportunity!

When you empower someone with work, education, or purpose, you not only give them a chance to better themselves, but also a chance to better their families, friends, and community.
