Event Information

The event hosts world class speakers across the nation for an afternoon of entertainment while enjoying a discussion about the services rendered by the organization. Proceeds from THE LUNCH promote Goodwill’s Workforce Development Programs, providing a wide-range of assessment, job training and career placement services.

Chew on This

  • Our Mission

    “Our Mission is to provide job training and employment to persons with disabilities and disadvantages to help transform their lives through the power of jobs.”

    When you empower someone with work, education, or purpose, you not only give them a chance to better themselves but also a chance to better their families, friends, and community.

Special Guests

General Stanly McChrystal, USA (Ret.)

Former Commander of U.S. and International Forces in Afghanistan, Former Chief of Staff of Military Operations in Afghanistan, Former leader of Joint Special Operations Command.

A one-of-a-kind commander with a remarkable record of achievement, General Stanley McChrystal is widely praised for creating a revolution in warfare that fused intelligence and operations.

Frances Townsend
Former Homeland Security Advisor to President George W. Bush.

From 2004 to 2008, Frances Townsend served as Assistant to President George W. Bush for Homeland Security and Counterterrorism and Chaired the Homeland Security Council.

For over 90 years Goodwill Dallas has been committed to providing job training and employment to those with disabilities and disadvantaging conditions.
